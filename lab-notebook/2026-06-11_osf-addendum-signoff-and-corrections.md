## 2. Decision: postura_proeminente = 'mixed' restricted to positivista + interpretativa

The summary field postura_proeminente previously collapsed any 2+ flag coactivation
into 'mixed'. Decision by F. Leite on 2026-06-11: 'mixed' is now reserved for the
combination positivista = 1 AND interpretativa = 1 (methodological pluralism in the
empirical sense). The doutrinario_normativa flag may coexist with either empirical
posture but does not trigger 'mixed' on its own.

New derivation rule (R code):
```r
postura_proeminente = case_when(
  epi_positivista_pred == 1 & epi_interpretativa_pred == 1 ~ 'mixed',
  epi_positivista_pred == 1                                ~ 'positivista',
  epi_interpretativa_pred == 1                            ~ 'interpretativa',
  epi_doutrinario_normativa_pred == 1                     ~ 'doutrinario_normativa',
  TRUE                                                    ~ 'inconclusivo'
)
```

Rationale: pos+dn articles (typical in Law cluster) would otherwise be grouped with
pos+int articles (typical in PA/STS), diluting the Law-doutrinario_normativa
association that H4 is designed to detect. The doutrinario_normativa presence is
preserved in the raw flags for any analysis requiring it.

Applied to: Manual v19 (R derivation block), Codebook v3.0 (postura_proeminente
schema), pilot/run_labels.py (commentary), pilot/llm_prepass.py (parse_response
logic for postura_proeminente_llm).
